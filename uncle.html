About Us

Welcome to MawSol Farms. We are dedicated to providing high-quality poultry products to our community. With a passion for farming and a commitment to sustainability, we ensure that our processes are ethical and our products are fresh. Our farm specializes in *spent layers*, which are chickens that have completed their egg-laying cycle. We believe in minimizing waste, so we provide these birds to individuals and businesses looking for nutritious meat options.

Our Products

  1. Spent Layers Our chickens that have completed their egg-laying cycle are available for purchase, providing a delicious and nutritious meat option.
  2. Fresh Eggs Collected daily, our eggs are known for their rich flavor and high nutritional value, making them a favorite among our customers.
  3. Processing Services We provide a complete service that includes killing, dressing, and delivering our poultry products straight to you, ensuring freshness and quality.
